# Crisp — App Store Listing

## App Name
Crisp

## Subtitle
Voice Recorder with AI

## Short Description (150 characters)
The smart voice recorder that transcribes, summarizes, and organizes your recordings with AI.

## Full Description (500 characters)
Crisp transforms your voice recordings into organized, searchable notes with AI-powered transcription and summaries.

KEY FEATURES:
• One-tap voice recording with high-quality audio
• Automatic AI transcription with speaker detection
• Smart summaries with keywords and action items
• Topic classification (Meeting, Personal, Idea, Tutorial, News, Health, Work)
• Sentiment analysis for emotional tone insights
• Organize with folders and favorites
• Crisp Links — share recordings as ephemeral links
• Cross-device sync via iCloud
• Beautiful dark mode design

PREMIUM FEATURES:
• Unlimited recordings
• Extended recording duration (up to 2 hours)
• Priority AI transcription
• Advanced editing tools
• Community sharing
