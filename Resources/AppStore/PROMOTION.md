## Press Release

FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

**Crisp Launches — The AI-Powered Voice Recorder That Thinks While You Talk**

Crisp transforms voice recordings into intelligent, organized notes with automatic AI transcription, smart summaries, topic classification, and sentiment analysis.

SAN FRANCISCO — March 25, 2026 — Today marks the launch of Crisp, a new voice recorder app that goes beyond simple recording to provide genuine AI-powered insights into your spoken content.

Unlike traditional voice recorders, Crisp automatically transcribes recordings, extracts key points and action items, classifies topics, and even analyzes the emotional tone of conversations. Users can share recordings instantly via Crisp Links — ephemeral URLs that expire after a set time or number of plays.

"We wanted to build the voice recorder we always wished existed," said the founder. "One that not only captures audio but actually helps you understand and act on it."

Crisp is available now on the App Store with a free tier (10 recordings/month) and premium plans starting at $4.99/month.
